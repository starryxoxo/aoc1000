**moagi** • *v.*
⎃ moh·A·gi

**Meaning**
To pass through; to let through.

**Usage**
"**Moagi** ang mga cutie."
(Let the cuties pass through)

**Origin**
Of Cebuano origin: *Moagi,* Agi
