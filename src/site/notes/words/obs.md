**obs** • *adv.*
Superlative: napaka(-)obs
Other variants: Ohbs, Ob
⎃ oHbs

**Meaning**
Stupid, unintelligent,  unintellectual

**Usage**
"Ang **obs** naman."
(*That's stupid*)

**Origin**
Of Filipino origin: *Bobo* (offensive), *stupid*
