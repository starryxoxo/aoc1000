# 01 — All About Love.

**AoC 1:1-5**
Love is a fundamental way of experiencing life.¹ For us humans, love is a necessity.² We don't feel *nor* won't *we be* human if we didn't receive love.³

Love exists in all kinds of dimensions and from all directions.⁴ It can come from yourself, your family, friends, and even romantic couples.⁵
